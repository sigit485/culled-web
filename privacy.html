Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

No data collected · 100% on-device

Culled is built around a simple principle: your photos are yours. This policy explains exactly what the app does and does not do with your information.

What we collect

Nothing. Culled does not collect, store, log, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics SDKs, and no advertising trackers.

How your photos are processed

All photo analysis — detecting blurry, dark, duplicate, and screenshot images — runs entirely on your device using Apple's on-device frameworks (Vision and Core Image). Your photos and their metadata never leave your iPhone and are never uploaded to any server. Culled has no backend.

Photo library access

Culled requests access to your photo library so it can scan for and, with your explicit confirmation, delete photos. Deletions use Apple's PhotoKit and move items to the system "Recently Deleted" album, where iOS keeps them recoverable for 30 days. Culled never deletes anything without your confirmation.

Subscriptions and payments

Culled Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details, Apple ID, or any personal information from these transactions. Subscription management is handled in your device Settings.

Third parties

Culled does not share data with third parties because it does not collect any data to share.

Children's privacy

Culled does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
