Where do deleted photos go?

To your iPhone's "Recently Deleted" album. iOS keeps them recoverable for 30 days, so nothing is ever lost instantly. Culled never deletes a photo without your confirmation.

Are my photos uploaded anywhere?

No. Every scan runs entirely on your device. Culled has no servers and collects no data.

Culled flagged a photo I want to keep.

Just swipe right (or tap the heart) to keep it. You can also lower detection sensitivity, or protect favorites and specific albums, under Settings.

What does Culled Pro include?

Unlimited deletions and monthly auto-scan. The free version lets you scan everything and delete up to 50 photos. Annual plans include a 7-day free trial.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Culled → Cancel. You keep Pro until the current period ends.

I restored my phone — how do I get Pro back?

Open Culled → Settings → tap "Restore Purchases."
